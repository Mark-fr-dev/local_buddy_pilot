Being a Buddy Is Easy

This guide explains the basic Local Buddy helper flow, what helpers do, and how helper applications work during the pilot.

1

Get a request

You’ll receive a message when someone needs help.

當有外國人需要幫助時，你會收到通知

2

Accept if you’re free

Choose jobs that fit your schedule.

自由選擇你有空的時間

3

Meet and help

Meet at the location and help with simple communication.

在指定地點見面，協助簡單溝通

4

Get paid

Help is done, then you get paid.

完成協助後即可獲得報酬

Helper application form

Use the form below to apply. During the pilot, helper applications are reviewed manually.

這個表單會收集您的聯絡資料、交通方式、語言能力、可配合時間，以及篩選相關資訊。

What Local Buddy helpers do

  • Support communication in everyday Chinese-language situations
  • Help customers ask questions and understand the process
  • Provide calm, practical support within clear service boundaries
  • Stay within the role of support, not professional advice or decision-making
