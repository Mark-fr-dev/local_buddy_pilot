FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What areas do you cover?

This pilot currently covers Hsinchu City and Zhubei only.

What kinds of tasks are allowed?

Everyday appointments, admin visits, phone calls during a session, and practical errands that fit within the pilot scope.

What can helpers not do?

Helpers cannot make decisions for clients, sign documents, handle money on behalf of clients, or provide legal or medical advice.
