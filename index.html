What Local Buddy is

Local Buddy is a helper service for foreigners in Taiwan who need practical support in everyday situations where Chinese is a barrier.

Human, calm, trustworthy, clear, and practical.

Who it is for

Local Buddy is for foreigners in Taiwan who have limited Chinese or feel stuck when everyday tasks become difficult because of language barriers.

What helpers can help with

  • Appointments and bookings
  • Banks and service counters
  • Housing and landlord communication
  • Shopping, returns, and delivery issues
  • Forms, messages, and everyday translation help
  • Clarifying information and asking questions

How it works

1

Make a booking request

Fill in the booking form with your task, location, preferred time, and contact details.

2

We review and confirm

We review your request, check that it fits the pilot, find a suitable helper, and contact you on LINE.

3

Meet your helper

Once payment is verified, your booking is confirmed and you meet your helper at the agreed location for practical support.

Important pilot notes

  • Hsinchu City and Zhubei only
  • Bookings must be made at least 2 hours in advance
  • Payment is required before the session
  • Booking is only finally confirmed after payment is verified
  • Helpers support communication and practical everyday tasks
  • Full service rules and boundaries are explained in the FAQ
