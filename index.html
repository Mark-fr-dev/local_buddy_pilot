Living in Taiwan is easier when you don’t have to do everything alone

Even after living here for years, many foreigners still run into situations where language, systems, and everyday admin become stressful. This service is designed to provide practical, in-person help when those situations become hard to manage alone.

What we help with

  • Appointments and admin visits
  • Bank-related tasks
  • Phone calls during a session
  • School or paperwork-related support
  • Scooter or practical errand assistance
  • Everyday translation support in real-world situations

Helpers provide language and practical support only. They do not make decisions for clients, sign documents, handle money, or provide legal or medical advice.

How it works

1

Submit a request

Tell us your time, location, and what you need help with.

2

We review it

We check the request manually and contact you to confirm the details.

3

Meet in person

You and your helper meet at the agreed location for the session.

Pilot service details

  • Hsinchu City and Zhubei only
  • In-person support only
  • 1-hour base session
  • 30-minute extensions if both sides agree
  • Short-notice bookings are not available during the pilot
  • Requests are reviewed manually before confirmation

This is a pilot service. Please be patient with us while we test and improve it.

Who this is for

This service is for foreigners in Hsinchu or Zhubei who want practical, in-person help with situations that are difficult to handle alone.
